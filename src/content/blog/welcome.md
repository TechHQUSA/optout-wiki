OptOut.wiki is a community-vetted directory of opt-out guides: step-by-step instructions for getting your data out of the hands of carmakers, data brokers, and apps that quietly share more than you'd like.

Every guide is sourced, dated, and re-tested regularly so the steps keep working as companies change their forms and settings. We're starting small: cars and telematics first, then expanding from there.

If you spot a step that's gone stale or a company we're missing, open a contribution. Vetting happens in the open.
