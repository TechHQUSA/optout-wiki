LinkedIn turns your profile, posts, and activity into training data for its generative-AI features, and the setting is on by default. Turning it off stops future use of your data for that purpose.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings & Privacy** from your profile menu.
2. Go to **Data privacy**.
3. Open **Data for Generative AI Improvement**.
4. Toggle **Use my data for training content-creation AI models** to **Off**.

The change applies going forward; LinkedIn states it does not undo past training.
